QMatrix

A picture may be worth a thousand words, but a well-crafted diagram is worth an entire book. Distill your knowledge, visualize your understanding, and share with the world. The world and our knowledge of it are compositional, so should be your notes. We aim to extract searchable data from video, audio, and texts, making it possible to learn and collaborate on knowledge instinctively. Qmatrix helps you command your information, collate your notes, video, audio, ideas, and experience into an instinctively intuitive format meant to help you understand and find the truth. Finally, it provides a way to collaborate and share knowledge to better help understanding.
